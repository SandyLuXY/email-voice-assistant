Dear Undergrad Students- The Big Data Summer Institute in Biostatistics, a SIBS program, is an interdisciplinary training and research program in biostatistics that introduces undergraduate students to the intersection of big data and human health — a rapidly growing field that uses quantitative analysis to help solve scientific problems and improve people’s lives.

The application for BDSI 2022 undergraduate research positions opens on Wednesday, December 1. Applications are reviewed on a rolling basis; you are encouraged to apply early.

The 2022 research program will be held from June 20 - July 29. Events take place on UM's campus. 

Please visit www.BigDataSummerInstitute.com for more information.
