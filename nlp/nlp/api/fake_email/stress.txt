Women in Electrical and Computer Engineering (WECE) and Society of Women Engineers (SWE) are teaming up with Wolverine Wellness to put on a virtual workshop on Stress Reduction and Overcoming Burnout! As the semester comes to a close, it's important to evaluate how we handle stress and burnout and the ways to address them. The event will take place on Thursday, December 2 at 6PM and will be virtual. Fill out this interest form to be emailed the Zoom link.
